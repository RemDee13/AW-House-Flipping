What we do

From foundation to finish

We buy tired, damaged houses and bring them all the way back — structural, mechanical and cosmetic, under one roof.

Roofing & Exterior

Full tear-offs, new decking, architectural shingles, chimney rebuilds and siding that shrugs off the next storm.

Windows & Doors

Energy-efficient Low-E windows, insulated entry doors and smart locks that make a house feel new the moment you walk up.

Plumbing & Systems

Full repipes, tankless water heaters, sewer scoping and the mechanical work buyers never see but always feel.

Landscaping & Yard

Regrading, fresh sod, fencing, gates and driveways — the curb appeal that turns a drive-by into a showing.

Full Renovations

One crew, one timeline, one number. We manage the entire flip so the home is move-in ready and market-bright.

About

Old bones, new life

Ashwood Revival exists to prove a simple idea: almost any house is worth saving. We specialize in the homes everyone else walks past — the leaking roofs, the boarded windows, the yards gone to weeds — and we bring them all the way back to life.

Every flip is run by one accountable crew with a fixed scope and a real timeline, so the work behind the walls is as honest as the finish in front of them.
